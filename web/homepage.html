Minecraft

This is a picture of my realm in Minecraft. I have been playing minecraft for many years, and built many different things. This realm is my current project. I share this realm with my family, some of which are out of state. The nice thing about a realm in Minecraft is that it is on a dedicated server, so it is cloud based. Anyone who has access to the realm can get on at anytime from anywhere and play. I also play other video games, but Minecraft is my main game I play.
